About Haladigadia

According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Haladigadia village is 400735. Haladigadia village is located in Korai tehsil of Jajapur district in Odisha, India. It is situated 4km away from sub-district headquarter Korai (tehsildar office) and 26km away from district headquarter Jajapur . As per 2009 stats, Sadakpur was the gram panchayat of Haladigadia village. Since 2015 Haldigadia nominated as Gram Panchayat.

The total geographical area of village is 183 hectares. Haladigadia has a total population of 1,626 peoples, out of which male population is 822 while female population is 804. This results in a sex ratio of approximately 978 females for every 1,000 males. Literacy rate of haladigadia village is 76.38% out of which 81.02% males and 71.64% females are literate. There are about 322 houses in haladigadia village. Pincode of haladigadia village locality is 755022.

Jajapur is nearest town to haladigadia village for all major economic activities, which is approximately 11km away.


Overview

Haladigadia Gram Panchayat is a Rural Local Body in Korei Panchayat Samiti part of Jajapur Zila Parishad. There are total 13 Villages under Haladigadia Gram Panchayat jurisdiction. Gram Panchayat Haladigadia is further divided into 11 Wards. Gram Panchayat Haladigadia has total 8 schools. Haladigadia Gram Panchayat has total 15 full time government employees.
