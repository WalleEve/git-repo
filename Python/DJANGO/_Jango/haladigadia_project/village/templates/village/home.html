Overview

Haladigadia Gram Panchayat is a Rural Local Body in Korei Panchayat Samiti, part of Jajapur Zila Parishad. There are a total of 7 villages under Haladigadia Gram Panchayat jurisdiction. Gram Panchayat Korei is further divided into 11 wards. Gram Panchayat Korei has a total of 8 schools and employs 15 full-time government employees.
